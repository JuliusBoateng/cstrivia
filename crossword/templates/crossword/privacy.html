Privacy

CS Trivia is a lightweight site built with privacy in mind.

This site does not track users, collect personal information, or use analytics. The code for the site is open source and available on GitHub.

To prevent puzzles from accidentally clearing on page refresh, your answers are temporarily stored in your browser's session storage. This data is automatically cleared by your browser when the tab is closed.
